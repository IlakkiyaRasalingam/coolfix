Our Services

Explore Our Services

AC Installation

Professional setup of new AC units, ensuring proper mounting, electrical connections, refrigerant levels, and testing for performance. Ideal for homes, offices, or commercial spaces needing fresh, efficient cooling.

AC Replacement

Removing old or damaged AC systems and installing new, energy-efficient units. Ensures improved performance, lower electricity bills, and better air quality with updated cooling technology.

AC Repair

Fixing common issues like no cooling, leaks, loud noises, sensor malfunctions, or system shutdowns. Quick diagnostics and reliable repairs restore your AC’s functionality and comfort.

AC Compressor Repair

Fixing common issues like no cooling, leaks, loud noises, sensor malfunctions, or system shutdowns. Quick diagnostics and reliable repairs restore your AC’s functionality and comfort.

Annual Maintenance (AMC)

Specialized service to diagnose and repair faulty or failed compressors. As the core of the cooling system, a healthy compressor ensures proper refrigerant flow and consistent cooling.

Coil Cleaning

Thorough cleaning of condenser and evaporator coils for improved cooling performance. Dirty coils reduce efficiency and strain the AC system, increasing energy consumption and wear.

Gas Refilling

Recharging refrigerant gas to the correct level for optimal cooling. Essential when AC blows warm air due to gas leakage or depletion, ensuring efficient and fast temperature control.
